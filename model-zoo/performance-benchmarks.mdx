## Training Strategy Impact

### Standard vs Extended Training

| Model Base | Standard Acc | Extended Acc | Improvement | Epochs |
|-----------|-------------|-------------|-------------|--------|
| ResNet-50 | 75.882% | 79.468% | +3.586% | 300 → 600 |
| ResNet-152 | 76.484% | 80.144% | +3.660% | 300 → 600 |
| DenseNet-121 | 73.612% | 77.302% | +3.690% | 300 → 600 |

**Key Insights:**
- Consistent ~3.6% improvement from extended training
- All extended models trained with AMP and EMA
- Significant gains justify the additional compute

## Compute Considerations

### Inference Speed (Relative)

Based on typical GPU inference characteristics:

**Fastest:**
1. ResNet-18, ResNet-34 (efficient convolutions)
2. DenseNet-121 (efficient architecture)
3. ResNet-50, ResNeXt-50

**Medium:**
1. ConvNeXt-Tiny
2. DenseNet-161/169/201
3. ResNet-101
4. ViT-C Tiny/Small (conv stem helps)

**Slower:**
1. ResNet-152
2. ConvNeXt-Base
3. ViT-C Base
4. Simple ViT Small/Base

**Slowest:**
1. ViT-C Large
2. Simple ViT Large
3. VGG-11 (large parameter count)
